MANUFACTURING UNIT

This is where we make our products which are 100% sustainable. The process that is involved in making starts from dying a plain cotton cloth with natural ingredients, after multiple washes we get the desired color. Printing takes place on this colored cloth where MITTI is applied to the area where prints are needed. Every product that is made by Anadeekala is a pure piece of real hardwork and dedication from our artisans.
